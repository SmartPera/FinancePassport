Frequently Asked Questions
Is SmartPera Passport free?
Yes — Passport and every tool inside it are 100% free, always.
Is my data safe?
Your account is protected by Firebase Authentication. We never collect your individual transactions — only summary scores, as explained during signup.
Why do I need to fill in my profile?
Your Country, Pass Type, and Income Range personalise every tool — currency, formulas, and recommendations are tailored to you automatically.
I moved countries — how do I update this?
Open Profile → Edit Profile, and update your Country. Everything else recalculates automatically.
Does Passport give financial advice?
No. Every calculator is educational and informational only — for personal financial advice, connect with a licensed advisor.
A tool isn't working — what do I do?
Message us on the WhatsApp Channel above with a screenshot. We read every message.
